Critical
Zero reviews on any product
›
Visited DON'T BE FOOLED Full Sleeve Tee (Rs. 2,199) — zero reviews, zero star ratings, no testimonials anywhere on the site

For Indian streetwear shoppers, social proof is everything. No reviews = no trust = no purchase.

Install Judge.me (free)Email all past buyers for reviewsOffer 10% off for review with photo
Critical
Subscription disclaimer on regular products
›
On regular tee product page: "This item is a deferred, subscription, or recurring purchase. By continuing, I agree to the cancellation policy..." — appearing on a one-time clothing purchase

This will scare buyers away. Nobody wants to accidentally sign up for a recurring charge on a tee shirt. This is a broken subscription app configuration.

Disable subscription app on regular productsAudit all product pages for this text
Critical
No size chart on product pages
›
Product pages show size selectors (S, M, L, XL, 2XL) with no size chart, no measurements, no fit guide. Description says "BOXY FIT" but gives zero measurements

Indian sizing varies widely. Without a chart, customers guess wrong, order the wrong size, and return. Returns are expensive and avoidable with one simple popup.

Add size chart modal next to size selectorInclude chest/length/shoulder measurementsAdd "Boxy fit — size down" note
Critical
No email/WhatsApp capture
›
No popup, no footer form, no WhatsApp opt-in anywhere on the entire site. The only contact option is a buried contact page

For Indian Gen Z streetwear buyers, WhatsApp + email marketing has the highest ROI. Every visitor who doesn't buy immediately is a lost lead forever — no way to re-engage them.

Add Klaviyo exit-intent popupAdd WhatsApp opt-in (Interakt/DelightChat)Offer 10% off first order for signup
Medium
Sold out items show with no restock option
›
3 products marked Sold Out on homepage with no "Notify Me" button: ONLY PLAN Z Baggy Pant (Rs. 2,468), ONLY PLAN Z Full Sleeve Tee (Rs. 2,468), Freehug Brown (Rs. 1,850)
Add Back-in-Stock notification appMove sold-out to end of gridShow "Notify Me" button on sold-out cards
Medium
COD not shown on product pages
›
"COD AVAILABLE" is in the announcement bar but not shown on product pages where the buying decision happens. No COD badge, no payment icons, no "pay on delivery" reassurance below Add to Cart

In India, COD is the #1 trust signal for online shopping. It's being hidden instead of celebrated.

Add COD badge below Add to Cart on all product pagesShow payment icons: COD + UPI + Cards
